Choral Folk Singers
Welcome, and thanks for your interest. Choral Folk Singers is a group of talented straight-tone singers preserving American folks music through contemporary, a cappella performance. We put on concerts, sing at wineries and breweries, weddings, and other events and love sharing our passion for melody and harmony with our audiences. To dive deeper into our world: take a listen, audition, or contact us for bookings and questions.
